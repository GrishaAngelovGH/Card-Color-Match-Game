How to Play

  • Match the top card by color or number.
  • Skip — next player loses their turn.
  • Reverse — change direction of play.
  • Draw Two — next player draws 2 and is skipped.
  • Wild — play on anything, choose a new color.
  • Wild Draw Four — choose color, next player draws 4 and is skipped.
  • If you can't play, draw a card. You may play it if it matches.
  • First player to empty their hand wins!
